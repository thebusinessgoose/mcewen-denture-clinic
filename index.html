About Us

Are you looking for an experienced denturist? Ken McEwen Denture Clinic provides caring, compassionate denture services in the Sechelt area. At Ken McEwen Denture Clinic, we strive to provide high-quality dentures around Sechelt, BC. With over 40 years of experience, we have all the necessary skills to personalize our services to your specific needs.
Whether you need a complete or partial denture, denture repairs, denture adjustments, our denturist, Ken McEwen can help fulfill your needs while keeping you at ease. To get your smile back, make an appointment with Ken McEwen Denture Clinic today!

We make all our dentures on site.

We also work closely alongside an independant Dental Hygienist, Monica Maletz, at Coastal Smiles. View her website here

All dental insurance plans are accepted.
